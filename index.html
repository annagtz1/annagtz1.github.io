NLP · Topic Modeling

Skill Demand in AI/ML Job Postings

Clustered large-scale AI/ML and data science job postings into distinct skill ecosystems using word embeddings and structural topic modeling, then trained a classifier that separates tech-focused postings with 0.97 AUC. Cloud and MLOps terms — AWS, Docker, Kubernetes — proved to be the strongest signal, not the LLM keywords everyone expected.

Team project comparing U.S. maternal mortality data against international peers and across demographic groups. An ANOVA test found a statistically significant relationship between race and maternal mortality rate (p < .05), and a five-number summary placed the U.S. at 17.40 deaths per 100,000 live births — over 4x the comparison group's median.
